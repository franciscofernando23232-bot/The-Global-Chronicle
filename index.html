World Affairs • Climate

Unprecedented June Heat Dome Shatters European Temperature Records

A historic "heat dome" has stalled directly over Western Europe, sending temperatures soaring to astronomical highs and breaking hundreds of local records. In France, Météo-France placed an unprecedented 72 departments under a red alert as the heat index peaked. Scientists from World Weather Attribution warn that this rapid-response event would have been virtually impossible without human-induced climate changes.

Ethics • Vatican Consistory

Pope Leo XIV Issues First Encyclical 'Magnifica Humanitas' on Artificial Intelligence

In an extraordinary assembly of the College of Cardinals in Rome, Pope Leo XIV promulgated his first encyclical, Magnifica Humanitas. Marking a dramatic step for the Church's doctrine, the encyclical establishes a formal framework on the protection of human dignity in the age of algorithmic expansion. The document urges international consensus on preserving manual human agency and warns against autonomous military technologies.

Ecclesial Directives

The Pope demands that AI systems remain strictly supplementary to human labor, stating that replacing human conscience with machines violates fundamental ethics.

A.I. Code

The Holy See establishes an ethics oversight committee to evaluate Catholic global organizations deploying neural networks.
